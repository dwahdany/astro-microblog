I don't know when I last (ever?) felt excited in the context of LaTeX. But tectonic just made me actually excited for that ecosystem. [](https://x.com/dwahdany)It's a self-contained Rust binary that comes with some nice things, like (by default) not writing out intermediate files and automatically doing the weird “run latex and bibtex for some number of times”-loop.
